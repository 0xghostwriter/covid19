Latest numbers

As of 2pm March 23, 2020 there are 2,102 positive cases and 40 deaths in California. Approximately 27,650 tests had been conducted. This includes the latest numbers California has received from commercial and private labs. At least 15,554 results have been received and another 12,100 are pending. See the California Department of Public Health (CDPH) for the latest data.

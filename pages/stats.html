Latest numbers

As of 2pm March 25, 2020 there are 3,006 positive cases and 65 deaths in California. Approximately 77,800 tests had been conducted. This includes the latest numbers California has received from commercial and private labs. At least 20,386 results have been received and another 57,400 are pending. See the California Department of Public Health (CDPH) for the latest data.

Statewide COVID-19 Hotline:

833-544-2374

The statewide call center will be open 7 days a week from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Pacific Daylight Time.  

This new COVID-19 information line is a part of our ongoing effort to provide reliable, trusted information and support.

Additionally, Californians can call their local 211 which are available 24 hours a day. 

We urge Californians to do the following:

  1. If you’re looking for general information about state COVID-19 resources: use covid19.ca.gov or call 833-544-2374 
  2. If you’re looking for community services and support: Call 211
  3. If you’re looking for medicine and medical attention: Call your healthcare provider or pharmacy

If it’s an emergency: Call 911

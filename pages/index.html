Here’s what everyone can do to keep communities healthy

People can spread this virus even if they never develop symptoms. Here’s how everyone can help keep communities healthy.

Do:

  • Wash hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • If soap and water aren’t available, use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol.
  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces.
  • Cough or sneeze into a tissue, then throw the tissue in the trash.

Don’t:

  • Shaking hands.
  • Touching your eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Contact with people who are sick.

What you can do

If you’ve been exposed or have symptoms

Stay home if you feel sick or you’ve been exposed.

If you’re at higher risk of illness

Stay home and monitor your health.

Testing and treatment

Call your healthcare provider for testing or treatment.

Cancel or postpone gatherings

Avoid gatherings.

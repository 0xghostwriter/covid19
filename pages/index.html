Coronavirus COVID-19 Response Portal

An outbreak of respiratory disease caused by a novel (new) coronavirus that has been detected in more than 100 locations internationally, including in the United States. The virus has been named “SARS-CoV-2” and the disease it causes has been named “coronavirus disease 2019” (abbreviated “COVID-19”).

Symptoms of COVID-19

The following symptoms may appear 2-14 days after exposure to the virus:

  • fever
  • cough
  • shortness of breath

I have symptoms

If you have symptoms, and

  • may have had contact with a person with COVID-19
  • or traveled recently to an area of concern

Contact your healthcare provider or your local health department

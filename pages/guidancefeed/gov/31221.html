First Partner Jennifer Siebel Newsom and California State Library Launch Five-Week Summer Reading Challenge 

SACRAMENTO — First Partner Jennifer Siebel Newsom today launched a five-week summer reading challenge designed to keep California’s families learning and engaged by “checking out” their community libraries. Families are encouraged to sign up for their local libraries’ summer reading programs, track the books they’re reading and share their progress on social media using the hashtag #CASummerReading. […]

The statewide call center will be open 7 days a week from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Pacific Daylight Time.  

Callers will be able to choose to be transferred directly to their local 2-1-1s, or to speak with a call center representative, available 24 hours a day. 

This new 800# COVID-19 information line is a part of our ongoing effort to streamline the information available to Californians and provide reliable, trusted information and support.

We urge Californians to do the following:

If you’re looking for General information about state COVID19 resources: Use covid19.ca.gov or call 833-544-2374 

If you’re looking for Community services and support: Call 211

If you’re looking for Medicine and medical attention: Call your health plan or pharmacy

If it’s an emergency: Call 911

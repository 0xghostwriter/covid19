Nursing homes and assisted living facilities nationwide have been severely affected by outbreaks of coronavirus. Senior living facilities are working with state and local health departments to reduce the disease’s impact on residents, families, and staff. 

There are new efforts to reduce infection– including new rules on family and friends visiting residents. Read them below, stay up to date on changes, and learn what you can do to protect and connect with your loved ones in senior living.

Facility information

To know what steps a nursing home or assisted living facility is taking, contact them or check their website. 

You may use the federal Nursing Home Compare to see detailed information about nursing homes.

You may use CDSS’s Care Facility Search to see assisted living facility complaints from the last 5 years, or lodge a complaint.

Bars required to close in certain counties

CA Public Health has announced the mandatory closure of bars for counties who have been on the County Monitoring List for more than 14 days. This includes the counties of Fresno, Imperial, Kern, Kings, Los Angeles, San Joaquin and Tulare. Some other counties are also recommended to close bars. See the complete list.

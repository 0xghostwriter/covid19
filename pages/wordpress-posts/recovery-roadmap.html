California is building a roadmap for modifying the statewide stay home order while maintaining community health and resiliency. We are currently in stage 1 of the resilience roadmap. When workplaces in each stage make the necessary modifications, we can move to the next stage. The state is currently issuing stage 1 guidance for essential sectors to get more people back to work with the necessary health and safety measures.

STAGE 1:
Safety and preparednes

Making workplaces safe for our essential workers
(Current stage)

STAGE 2:
Lower-risk workplaces

Adapting retail, offices, schools, and child care so they can be reopened safely

STAGE 3:
Higher-risk workplaces

Adapting salons, movie theaters, sports, and religious services so they can be reopened safely

STAGE 4:
End of Stay Home Order

When treatments are available, reopen areas of highest risk: concerts, conventions, sports arenas

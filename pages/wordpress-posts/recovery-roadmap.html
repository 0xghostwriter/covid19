California is building a roadmap for modifying the statewide Stay Home Order while maintaining community health and resiliency. When each stage is complete, we can move to the next one, until pandemic conditions have passed and restrictions are lifted.

STAGE 1: Safety and preparedness (Current stage)

Making workplaces safe for our essential workers

STAGE 2: Lower-risk workplaces

Adapting retail, offices, schools, and child care so they can be reopened safely

STAGE 3: Higher-risk workplaces

Adapting salons, movie theaters, sports, and religious services so they can be reopened safely

STAGE 4: End of Stay Home Order

When treatments are available, reopen areas of highest risk: concerts, conventions, sports arenas

Reopening guidelines

California has issued guidance for each sector of the economy to get essential workers back on the job. To provide your input on future guidance, fill out the California Recovery Roadmap survey.
